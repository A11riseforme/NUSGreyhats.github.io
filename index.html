Cross CTF

Cross CTF is a cyber security competition organized annually where teams from the tertiary institutions are welcome to compete for the coveted trophy. Cross CTF 2017 is organized by SMU Whitehat Society and NUS Greyhats this year.

Realistic Challenges

Challenges in the competition are designed to be as realistic and similar to real-life scenarios as possible.

CTF 101

Capture The Flag 101 is a capture-the-flag (CTF) style workshop that will be spanned over two days. Participants will learn techniques and tools to exploit software vulnerabilities to uncover secret flags hidden in various challenge. This workshop is designed to be progressive and is beginner friendly.
